Arroz con Gandules

Description

A classic Puerto Rican dish. Rice and beans with an added kick from sofrito and annatto seeds. Tastes great paired with chicken, but this dish has a very full flavor that holds up on its own too.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup canola oil
  • 3/4 cups annatto seeds
  • 2 cups rice
  • 2 cans gandules
  • 1 cup sofrito
  • 4 cups water
  • 3 packets Goya Chicken Bouillon

Steps

  1. Heat canola oil over high flame.
  2. Add annatto seeds to oil.
  3. Once oil is deep red, strain seeds and pour oil into cooking pot.
  4. Add rice to heated oil.
  5. Cook for 5 minutes over high flame, constantly stirring.
  6. Drain gandules (save excess liquid and use as part of your 4 cups of water) and pour them into rice. Stir until mixed.
  7. Add sofrito and stir until mixed.
  8. Let cook for 3 minutes.
  9. Pour 4 cups of water into pot.Stir until completely mixed.
  10. Add 3 packets of chicken bouillon.
  11. Cover and cook on low heat for 15-20 minutes.
  12. Switch to low heat, uncover, and stir.
  13. Cook for 5-10 minutes (uncovered), occasionally stirring.
