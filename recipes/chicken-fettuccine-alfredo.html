Chicken Fettuccine Alfredo

Description

An Italian dish with a creamy yet light sauce that clings to the pasta perfectly. The addition of garlic and parmesan cheese add a strong, savory flavor. Chicken can be served alongside the pasta or mixed in.

Ingredients

  • 2 chicken breasts
  • 1/2 tsp garlic powder
  • salt
  • pepper
  • flour
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 3 tbsp butter
  • 8 oz fettuccine
  • 4 oz cream cheese
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1/4 cup chicken broth
  • 2 large cloves of garlic (minced)
  • 1 cup parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Soften cream cheese outside of refrigerator for 30 minutes.
  2. Grate parmesan cheese.
  3. Boil a large pot of water.
  4. Cook fettuccine al dente according to package instructions.
  5. While fettuccine cooks, cut chicken in half lengthwise (resulting in 4 cutlets).
  6. Sprinkle salt, pepper, and garlic powder onto both sides of chicken breasts and coat them in flour.
  7. Add olive oil and 1 tbsp butter to skillet on medium high heat.
  8. Once pan is hot, add chicken and cook for 5 minutes on each side or until cooked through. Plate the chicken and rinse the skillet.
  9. Add remaining butter, cream cheese, chicken broth, and garlic to skillet and place on medium heat.
  10. Once butter has melted, use spoon to help cream cheese melt into the sauce for a couple of minutes. Once it has, let sauce bubble for 5 minutes, occasionally stirring.
  11. Slice chicken into strips while sauce bubbles.
  12. Stir parmesan cheese into sauce and let cook for a minute. Taste and add salt and pepper as needed.
  13. Drain fettuccine and add to skillet. Toss it with the sauce.
